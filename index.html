Mathematical Model

Model description goes here Let $V$ be the set of vertices in our graph and $E$ the set of edges. We define the variables \[ x_v = \left\{\begin{array}{ll} 1 & \text{if $v \in V$ is selected}\\ 0 & \mathrm{otherwise} \end{array}\right. \] The minimal edge cover is given by the following model \[ \begin{array}{ll} \text{minimize} & \sum_{v \in V} x_v \\ \text{subject to} & x_u + x_v \ge 1, \quad \forall (u,v) \in E \\ & x_v \in \{ 0, 1 \} \quad \forall v \in E \end{array} \]
